Dune

"Dune" is a classic science fiction novel by Frank Herbert about a young nobleman named Paul Atreides who becomes embroiled in a power struggle on a desert planet called Arrakis, where a valuable resource known as spice is produced.

Price: £20

Malazan - Book of the fallen

"Malazan Book of the Fallen" is a high fantasy series by Steven Erikson that follows the epic and intricate stories of various characters across multiple continents, timelines, and dimensions. The series is known for its complex plotlines, worldbuilding, and philosophical themes.

Price: £25

The witcher series

"The Witcher" is a series of fantasy novels and short stories by Polish author Andrzej Sapkowski that follows the adventures of Geralt of Rivia, a witcher, or monster hunter, in a medieval-inspired world filled with magic, politics, and complex moral dilemmas.

Price: £26

Percy jackson

"Percy Jackson and the Olympians" is a series of young adult fantasy novels by Rick Riordan that follows the adventures of Percy Jackson, a demigod, and his friends as they navigate a world filled with Greek gods, monsters, and magic.

Price: £27

Vagabond

"Vagabond" is a Japanese historical fiction manga series by Takehiko Inoue that is loosely based on the life of the legendary swordsman Miyamoto Musashi. The manga follows Musashi's journey as he seeks to perfect his swordsmanship and find meaning in life through various conflicts and encounters with other historical figures. Known for its detailed artwork and portrayal of Japanese culture and history, "Vagabond" has become a critically acclaimed and popular manga series.

Price: £10

Ender's game

"Ender's Game" is a science fiction novel by Orson Scott Card about a young boy named Ender Wiggin who is recruited to attend Battle School to prepare for an alien invasion. The novel explores themes of war, leadership, and ethics through Ender's rigorous training and moral dilemmas.

Price: £20

The expanse series

"The Expanse" is a sci-fi series by James S. A. Corey that follows a diverse group of characters navigating political tensions and conspiracies in a future where humanity has colonized the solar system, and face threats that could destroy everything they know. The series has been adapted into a popular TV show..

Price: £27

The wheel of time

"The Wheel of Time" is a high fantasy series by Robert Jordan (and later Brandon Sanderson) that takes place in a world where magic exists and follows a group of characters as they become embroiled in a battle between good and evil, with the fate of the world hanging in the balance. The series has gained a large following for its intricate world-building, extensive cast of characters, and epic storytelling.

Price: £27

Red rising

"Red Rising" is a science fiction novel by Pierce Brown that takes place in a dystopian future society on Mars, where a caste system divides the population into color-coded groups based on their genetic make-up. The story follows a young man named Darrow, who infiltrates the ruling class of Golds to bring about a revolution and overthrow the oppressive society. The novel has been praised for its fast-paced plot, vivid world-building, and complex characters.

Price: £57
